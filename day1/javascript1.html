Why Sherlock Holmes is the Best Fictional Character

  • He helps others with their problems—even with no intention of financial gain on his part!
  • He has appreciation for the arts: he plays violin, attends concerts, and frequently dresses up in a variety of costumes
  • He is very fashionable
  • People were so affected by his death—they continued to write letters to Arthur Conan Doyle insulting him and demanding he undid what he wrote.
  • Throughout the past 137 years, Sherlock holmes has been a character to inspire and entertain millions and millions of people.
